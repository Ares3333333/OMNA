# Omna Growth and Monetization

Omna should be sold as a living digital ritual, not as a meditation app.

## Positioning

Short formula:

> Omna is a living sound held by people. Enter for three minutes and leave your wave.

The promise is not productivity, therapy, or spiritual status. The promise is a short, beautiful act of shared presence.

## Distribution Loops

### 1. Invite loop

Every completed session should invite one simple action:

- "Позвать человека"
- share text with current link
- no account wall
- no long explanation

Goal: one session creates one possible new session.

### 2. Daily ritual loop

The 22:00 ritual is the return reason:

- countdown before the ritual
- live state during the ritual
- social posts should always point to "сегодня в 22:00"

Goal: Omna becomes a daily appointment, not a random website.

### 3. Short-form content

Make 10-20 second videos:

- close-up of the sphere breathing
- screen recording with sound
- one sentence: "Сегодня в 22:00 держим один звук"
- CTA: "Войди в Omna"

Do not explain too much. Mystery converts better here than instruction.

### 4. Telegram channel

Create a tiny channel:

- daily reminder at 21:50
- one link
- one visual
- no community chatter at first

Later this can become a Telegram Mini App, but not before the web ritual has retention.

### 5. Offline QR

Use QR cards/posters in:

- sound healing / breathwork spaces
- small galleries
- tea spaces
- yoga studios without using yoga clichés in Omna itself
- ambient music events

The QR should say: "Войди в общий звук".

## Monetization Ladder

### Stage 1: Supporter payment

Keep the core ritual free.

Add a soft supporter link:

- "Поддержать Omna"
- one-time payment
- monthly supporter
- no locked core experience

This tests whether people feel enough meaning to pay.

### Stage 2: Paid ritual evenings

Offer occasional guided events:

- 20-30 minute online ritual
- limited time
- hosted by founder / artist / voice guide
- payment by link

This is easier to sell than an abstract subscription.

### Stage 3: Private circles

For small groups:

- private Omna room
- shared link
- session summary
- optional facilitator mode

Customers:

- therapists
- breathwork facilitators
- retreat hosts
- creative teams
- online communities

### Stage 4: B2B ritual product

For teams and communities:

- "3-minute collective reset"
- branded/private room
- monthly plan
- no medical claims

Sell it as calm shared presence, not healthcare.

## Pricing Hypotheses

Start simple:

- Supporter: 299-499 RUB/month or $5/month
- One paid ritual: 300-900 RUB or $7-15
- Private circle: 1,500-5,000 RUB/month or $19-49/month
- B2B/team: from 9,900 RUB/month or $99/month

These are hypotheses. Validate with real users before building heavy billing.

## What Not To Do

- Do not add registration before users ask for persistence.
- Do not lock the main ritual behind payment.
- Do not make Omna look like a course platform.
- Do not use wellness clichés.
- Do not claim medical or therapeutic outcomes.
- Do not over-explain the product in the interface.

## Next Business Experiment

Run a 7-day launch:

1. Post one visual/video per day.
2. Invite people to join at 22:00.
3. Ask after session: "Что ты почувствовал?"
4. Track shares, returns, session length, and supporter clicks.
5. Only then decide what to monetize first.
